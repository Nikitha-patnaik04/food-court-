Our Story

Recipe Finder was born in 2020 from a simple idea: to make cooking accessible and enjoyable for everyone, regardless of their skill level. What started as a small collection of family recipes has grown into a thriving community of food enthusiasts sharing their culinary creations.

Our founder, Sarah Johnson, began by documenting her grandmother's recipes that were passed down through generations. She realized these treasured dishes could help others create meaningful connections through food, just as they had in her family.

Today, Recipe Finder features thousands of recipes from home cooks and professional chefs around the world, all united by a passion for good food and the joy of cooking.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To empower people to cook with confidence by providing reliable, tested recipes and cooking guidance that makes home cooking accessible to everyone.

We believe that cooking is not just about feeding ourselves, but about creativity, connection, and celebration. Our mission is to remove the intimidation factor from cooking and replace it with inspiration.

Our Vision

To build the world's most trusted and comprehensive recipe resource, where anyone can find the perfect recipe for any occasion, skill level, or dietary need.

We envision a world where people value home cooking as a way to nourish both body and soul, and where recipe sharing builds bridges between cultures and generations.
